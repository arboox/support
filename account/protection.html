Actions to ensure account protection


Your ARBOOX account gives you access to all of our apps and services. It is also the key to posting content in the ARBOOX app.




How to protect your account


There are two main recommendations to keep your account safe from attacks or identity thefts:


+Pick a strong password: Use a combination of numbers, letters and special characters to build up a strong password.

+Never share your credentials: To prevent identity damage, never share your password. When doing so, share it with trusted people only, or people within your organization/community.




My account has been stolen. What can I do?


If someone else has accessed your ARBOOX account, you can try reestablishing your password. Openarboox.com/setup/reestablishand pick a stronger password. If this doesn't work, you can request a security copy and delete your account forever atarboox.com/setup/delete.
