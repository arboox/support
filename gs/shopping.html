Shopping in augmented reality


ARBOOX Shopping helps you preview and purchase products from a wide variety of globally-famous brands. View real products in real environments with the help of augmented reality.




How to shop


Locate the app tab bar at the bottom of your device's screen. Look up for the  icon. If you can't find this icon, you may be in an unsupported region.


The shop shows you the most popular products and brands, updated weekly. If you find a product of your interest, click on it and you'll trigger a detail page. From here, you can view product pictures, read its description and consult pricing. To preview this product in augmented reality click on  Preview in AR.
