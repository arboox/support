Immersing in an AR experience


ARBOOX is an augmented reality app based on a card user interface. To launch an AR experience, just tap on a card from the Feed or Explore sections.




How to pick an experience


You can find augmented reality experiences in the Feed () or Explore () screens in the ARBOOX app. New experiences are constantly added, but to see them all you must access the Experiences screen (to do so go to Explore > Popular experiences > View all). To open the AR experience, just tap the card.

How to use the AR viewer


ARBOOX's advanced AR viewer is easy-to-use. It allows you to view experience content in both 3D and augmented reality. To view expeirences in AR just tap the Activate AR button.


Additionally, you can do the following from the viewer:


+Like AR experiences

+Play sounds/animations

+Share experiences (even with people who are not ARBOOX users)


To close the AR viewer, locate the  at the top right corner of your screen.




 NOTE: You must grant ARBOOX permission to your camera for AR experiences to work.
