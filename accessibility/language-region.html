Change app language or region


Easily change the content displaying in the app by switching your language or region.




How to change language/region


You can access the region settings on every app screen by tapping the  icon. This will trigger the options sheet. Scroll to the very bottom of it and pressLANGUAGE (REGION)



Please note that some regions offer limited content due to restrictive policies. In some cases, you may not be able to access ARBOOX from specific regions.




Additional information


Default language and region for ARBOOX is English (Australia).
