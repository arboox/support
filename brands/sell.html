Selling products in ARBOOX


Brands can sell their merchandise in the ARBOOX app. Users can preview select products in augmented reality. To set up a new product listing, locate the  icon . Press it and then select  Upload product. Provide the necessary information to personalise your product listing, and then tap thePublishbutton to make your product publicly available.




Additional information


You must be part of the ARBOOX For Brands programme to use Brand Dashboard. Apply atarboox.com/brands.
