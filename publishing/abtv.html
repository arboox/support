Uploading to ABTV


Publish your own videos to ARBOOX TV. Add tags to increase content exposure and build a viewer community.




How to upload your video to ABTV


Tap the  icon in any of the app's screens. Select  ABTV. From the ABTV Upload screen, upload a video from a valid URL, and add tags to increase exposure. Once you're done composing, press thePostbutton. You will be prompted to confirm your credentials (username and password) to publish the post.
